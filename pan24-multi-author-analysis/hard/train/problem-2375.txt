Used clothing has become an odd form of "dumping" that happens to crush any ability for a domestic industry. Since people are getting cloths for next to free, there's no way for a domestic industry to form. Because of this, a lot of countries (including Indonesia) have banned the import of used clothing.
This company got around it by setting up a business that would collect shoes and then turn them as a sand and rock alternative for children's parks. But then they had a black market used shoe operation that would export used shoes for profit. Dow seems to have gotten themselves into some weird black market niche.
